    What Is A Migraine

    Migraine is one form of headache which is painful. The pain it causes is accompanied by throbs usually on one side of the head alongside other symptoms like nausea and sensitivity to light. When sufferers lie down calmly in a dark room, they seem to get relief. The cause of migraine attack is not clear but the triggers are identifiable; such as stress, periods, tiredness and certain foods or drinks.

    Causes


    • Head trauma

    • Hormonal chages

    • Lack of sleep

    • Certain medicines
